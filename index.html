My Philosophy

I believe that websites are a unique way of portraying the purpose and personality behind a business or organization. Websites are not just an online billboard, they are more like a showroom, salesman, and personal representative all thrown into one. People who visit your website should be able to get an accurate feel for the personality behind what you do and what you are about. Whether it's positive or negative, your visitors will form an opinion of your organization based on your website. Make sure it's a good one!

What I Do

I work to build websites that meet my clients specific needs and unique situation. The options can range from basic static websites to custom WordPress sites to full on custom web applications. I can design and build the website for you, but I also like to include you in a big way in the design and planning process! Having you involved is the best way to ensure that you are getting a website that will suit you.

My Process

I believe strongly in involving you heavily in the design and planning phase of the project. The process is much like storyboarding for films. The first phase involves me learning as much as I can about your organization so that I can accurately depict the personality and details, followed by hashing out details like who will be providing content such as pictures and written information, whether that is you or me. I'll make low details sketches of the website as I lay out the different pages and the content and features that will go on each page. Then I'll work on giving you a unique look and feel as I get your input on colors, pictures, font styles, and so on. You'll be able to see a high detailed representation of what your website will look like before I even build it. Once you are satisfied with how it looks, I'll take over and do the rest of the heavy lifting!
