My Philosophy

I believe that websites are a unique way of portraying the purpose and personality behind a business or organization. Websites are not just an online billboard, they are more like a showroom, salesman, and personal representative all thrown into one. People who visit your website should be able to get an accurate feel for the personality behind what you do and what you are about. Whether it's positive or negative, your visitors will form an opinion of your organization based on your website. Make sure it's a good one!

What I Do

To me websites are a blank canvas through which a person can express themselves and portray their organization in a way that accurately reflects what they are all about. I like to help people find the solution that best meets this goal. That could involve anything from a basic website to a fully customizable WordPress site to a completely unique web application. I can do everything from basic logo design to setting up database-backed applications.
